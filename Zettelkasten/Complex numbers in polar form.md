[[Complex Numbers]]
$z=-2+2\sqrt{3}$ $\rightarrow$ $(-2,2\sqrt{3})$
Coordinates are in Quadrant 2

Radius
$r=\sqrt{(x)^2+(y)^2}$
$r=\sqrt{(-2)^2+(2\sqrt{3})^2}$
$r=\sqrt{4+(4*3)}$
$r=\sqrt{4+12}$
$r=\sqrt{16}$
$r=4$

Theta
$tan\theta=\frac{2\sqrt{3}}{-2}$
$tan\theta=-\sqrt3$
$\theta=arctan(-\sqrt{3})$
$\theta=\frac{2\pi}{3}$
