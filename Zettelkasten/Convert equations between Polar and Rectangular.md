## Polar$\rightarrow$Rectangular
$r=1$
$x^2+y^2=1$

$\theta=(\frac{\pi}{6})$
$tan(\frac{\pi}{6})=\frac{y}{x}$
$\frac{-\sqrt{3}}{3}=\frac{y}{x}$
$y=\frac{-\sqrt{3}}{3}*x$

## Rectangular$\rightarrow$Polar
$x+y=1$
$rcos\theta+rsin\theta=1$
$r(cos\theta+sin\theta) = 1$
$r=\frac{1}{(cos\theta+sin\theta)}$
