Equations with an extra parameter, normally referred to as time

## Examples

$x=\sqrt{t+3}$ and $y=2t$
Eliminate the parameter:
$x=\sqrt{t+3}$
$x^2=(\sqrt{t+3})^2$
$x^2=t+3$
$x^2-3=t$
$t=x^2-3$

Plug into y equation
$y=2t$
$y=2(x^2-3)$
$y=2x^2-6$ 
Final Equation:
$y=2x^2-6$
