Exponents are a small raised number within a mathematical statement to denote to enact repeated multiplication.

$2^0 = 1$
$2^1 = 2 = 2$
$2^2 = 2*2 = 4$
$2^3 = 2*2*2 = 8$

$2^{-2} = 1/2^2 = 1/4$

#### Exponent Rules
Multiplication
$x^a * x^b = x^{a+b}$
$x^2*x^3=x^5$
$x^2*x^3=x^{(2+3)}$

Division
$x^a/x^b=x^{(a-b)}$
$x^3/x^2=x^{(3-2)}$

Parentheses 
$(x^a)^b=x^{(a*b)}$
