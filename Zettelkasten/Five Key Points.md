These are all obvious coordinate points on the [[Unit Circle]]
These key points will help with [[Graphing Sine & Cosine.canvas|Graphing Sine & Cosine]]* and [[Graphing Secant & Cosecant.canvas|Graphing Secant & Cosecant

[[Sine]]:
(0 , 0)
($\frac {\pi} {2}$ , 1)
($\pi$ , 0)
($\frac {3\pi} {2}$ , -1)
($2\pi$ , 0)

[[Cosine]]:
(0 , 1)
($\frac {\pi} {2}$ , 0)
($\pi$ , -1)
($\frac {3\pi} {2}$ , 0)
($2\pi$ , 1)
